Our Vision

Milano, a city that has witnessed the rise and fall of empires, the birth of artistic masterpieces, and the evolution of modern civilization, deserves to have its rich history preserved and shared with the world in the most immersive way possible.

Our Historical Digital Twin project was born from a passion to bridge the gap between past and present, allowing visitors to experience Milan's transformation through the ages. From its origins as the Roman Mediolanum to its current status as a global fashion and design capital, every era tells a story worth preserving.

We believe that history should not be confined to textbooks. Through cutting-edge digital technology and meticulous historical research, we're creating an interactive experience that brings Milan's past to life.

This platform serves as a digital repository of knowledge, a virtual museum, and an educational tool that makes Milan's incredible heritage accessible to researchers, students, tourists, and history enthusiasts worldwide.
